About Us

The Metal House is a small online record store that was started with nothing but ideas and a passion. There is no doubt that modern music streaming platforms have risen to prominance over the past few decades, forcing physical music to slowly fade out. The Metal House was created to show love to these physical forms of music, mainly vinyls. We hope to show the world that physical music is still an awesome way to jam to your favorite tunes!

Much love to all our customers
